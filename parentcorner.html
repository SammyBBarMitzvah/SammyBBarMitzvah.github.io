PARENT'S CORNER



Dear Parents,

We are very much looking forward to having your child attend
Sammy’s Bar Mitzvah on October 20th! For those of you who have never
attended a Bar Mitzvah, we thought we would answer a
few frequently asked questions.

What is a Bar Mitzvah?
When a Jewish child reaches the age of thirteen, he is
recognized as an adult in the Jewish community. One great
honor that accompanies becoming an adult is the opportunity
to lead a Shabbat service. Sammy has studied the Hebrew
language for the past seven years and has been preparing for
this honor and privilege for the past few months.


What should my child do at the service
The service will begin at 9:00am, however, your child does not
have to arrive until 9:45am. Upon arrival, they should quietly
take a seat in the sanctuary, although the boys should make sure to
pick up a kippah (traditional Jewish head covering) before
entry to the sanctuary. Although this is a religious service,
your child will not be called upon to do anything more than
observe and perhaps read along with the Congregation, if he or
she is comfortable doing so. Girls should wear a dress or skirt
(no bare shoulders) and boys should wear nice pants and a
collared shirt (no jeans, please). Cell phones must be turned off
before entering the synagogue. The service should conclude
around 12pm, followed by a Kiddush luncheon afterwards. Your
child is welcome to stay and grab a bite to eat or leave when
the service concludes – it’s entirely up to them!


Anything I should know about the party?
Yes! The party starts at 7pm and your child should come ready
to have a great time! We have planned for an awesome experience
with lots of music, dancing, games, food, and FUN for everyone!
All guests are encouraged to wear sports jerseys (if they have
them), to go along with our party’s football theme. If your child
does not have a sports jersey - that’s ok - they can wear any
dressy casual attire that makes them comfortable and will fit
right in!


When is the party over?
You should anticipate picking up your child anywhere from
11:15pm – 12:00am, depending on the how much fun they're
having! We recognize not everyone is a partying night owl,
so please stay in contact with your child throughout the
evening in case they wish to leave early.


We look forward to sharing this event with your child!!
