Ph: (301) 590-0044

Hotel Website

For our friends and family who are coming in from out of town (or for those of you who just might want a hotel room for the evening!), we have arranged a hotel room block at the Gaithersburg Marriott for $79/night. The Marriott is in a prime location with food/shopping options and is approximately 15 minutes from the synagogue and 30 minutes from the party venue. Parking and wifi are included in the nightly rate, however, they cannot guarantee bed type, so please make sure to note your preference when making the reservation.
