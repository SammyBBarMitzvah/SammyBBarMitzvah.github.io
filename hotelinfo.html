9751 Washingtonian Blvd., Gaithersburg, MD 20878

Ph: (301) 590-0044

For our friends and family who are coming in from out of town (or for those of you who just might want
a hotel room for the evening!), we have arranged a hotel room block at the Gaithersburg Marriott for
$79/night. The Marriott is in a prime location with food/shopping options and is approximately 15
minutes from the synagogue and 30 minutes from the party venue. Parking and wifi are included in the
nightly rate, however, they cannot guarantee bed type, so please make sure to make note of your
preference when making the reservation.


Please use this link to make your reservation.


If you feel that you need to call and make your reservations, please call 1-800-393-3450 and refer to the
Beck Bar Mitzvah Room Block for October 19 - 21, 2018.


Regardless of how you make your reservation, we suggest you do it soon because there are limited
rooms in our block and the rate will most definitely go up once they're gone!
